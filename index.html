I decided to pursue further education because I felt the need for a change. My choice led me down the path of becoming a Front End Developer. I attended a high school where we dabbled in web design, but back then, I didn't fully embrace it. However, my interest in the coding world has grown significantly over time, and I now consider it an incredible and valuable skill to acquire, especially in this digital age where everything is becoming increasingly digitized.

To Grow in Web Design
